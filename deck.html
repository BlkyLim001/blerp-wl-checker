Trust is Valuable.
But Not Tradable.

  • No way to Short a failing leader.

The Solution

If You Can Price It,
You Can Trade It.

RepDex financializes social reputation.

  • Track: Real-time sentiment charts.
  • Earn: Creators stake their own scores.
